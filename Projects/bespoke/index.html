Work on our unfinished robots or bring your own idea to the table!

Have a good idea? Life changing invention? Don't know where to start? Enter your idea with our bespoke project! What we provide:

  • Advise from our experts on your project

  • Funding and components

  • Access to equipment and departmental machines (3D printer, PCB manufacturing, storage space, etc.)

You must submit an application to the society concerning your goals and budget, we will check:

  • Feasibility of the project,

  • Doable within a timeframe ( 1 year or 24 weeks, not including holidays)

  • If it is worth the society's budget

  • Committee member will have a conversation with you to assess if your capable
