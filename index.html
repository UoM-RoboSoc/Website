We are

ROBOSOC is a student-led robotics society based at the University of Manchester, which aims to encourage students of all levels to get involved in robotics. Since 2013, the Robotics Society offers a great opportunity to develop your practical skills outside of the course curriculum; you'll be able to build exciting projects with state-of-the-art technology in a relaxed environment.

Unlock Your Robotics Journey

Beginner to Advanced

Projects are available to those who need to learn the absolute basics, to those with extensive knowledge of the field.

Affordable robotics access

We enable use of university facilities, can buy components for members where feasible, and have never charged membership fees.

Meet like-minded people

We want to be a place where members can make friends and connections that will stay with them of the rest of their lives.
